Amber Restaurant Journey


Amber was founded in 2006 by Alexander, Amber opened their first restaurant in Uptown Nakuru, In 2007, Amber opened a branch in Nanyuki followed by their branch in Eldoret, and then Amber Express in Garden city Mall. In 2015, Amber opened their largest restaurant in Nakuru with a capacity of 600 people. It was launched in March 2015

Visit any of our branches to enjoy our traditional and mordern meals. We are opened 24/7. Get the best and leave with a smile to come again
